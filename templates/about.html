Your Platform - We run entirely online, and entirely based on your feedback.

  1. Moderation - We intend to moderate discussion in Congress and force a return to rational discourse, with a focus on multi-partisan solutions and fact-checking.
  2. Transparency - We would like to accelerate FOIA requests, install civilian oversight, and provide a safe framework that encourages responsible whistle-blowing.
  3. Campaign Finance Reform - We will refuse all corporate donations over $1,000, instead relying on social media, viral marketing, and original content.
  4. Scientific Inquiry - With a focus on keeping an open mind, sustainable living, and humane exploration.
  5. Ballot Access - Our proposed solution is a hybrid system.
  6. Hypothesis: The #1 priority is to disrupt the Two-Party System and return to a fluid majority in Congress. This is the root cause of our government's dysfunction and this proposal will help illustrate that notion.
  7. Further Development - I personally identify as Libertarian and Green. I believe in strong individual rights, strong state's rights, and sustainable living. For divisive issues, I intend to experiment with crowd-sourced solutions, notably: liquid feedback, loomio, and citizen juries. More on emerging discourse platforms.
  8. Branching - You are encouraged to use any or all of this platform as a starting point for your own campaign. Everything is available in our GitHub Repo under an MIT license.

Mission Statement - The Third Coalition is primarily concerned with co-ordinating discussion between Third Parties and Independents. We are also on a mission to educate the public about Electoral Reform. Our first challenge is to defeat the entrenched Two-Party System.

About Us - The Direct Democracy Party (DDP) was founded by Andrew Warshaver and Jeffrey Schroder in late 2014. Our Indiegogo Campaign is still live. Read more about us on our AMA. The Third Coalition is an agile pivot from the original plan.
