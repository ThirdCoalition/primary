Your Platform - We run entirely online, and entirely based on your feedback.

  1. Moderation - We intend to moderate discussion in Congress and force a return to rational discourse.
  2. Transparency - We would like to accelerate FOIA requests, install civilian oversight, and provide amnesty for whistle blowers. Our own transparency measures: check out our cartoons to understand my viewpoint. Check out our almanac to see the world through my eyes (and learn how to predict the future).
  3. Campaign Finance Reform - Pretty self explanatory but could use suggestions on specifics.
  4. Electoral Reform - Our favorites are Range and Proportional. Read about even more types here.
  5. Scientific Inquiry - With a focus on listening to all opinions, consensus building, sustainable living, and fact-checking.
  6. Hypothesis: The #1 priority is to disrupt the Two-Party System and return to a fluid majority in Congress. This is the root cause of our government's dysfunction and We are working on a proposal to illustrate this notion further.
  7. Further Development - I personally identify as Libertarian and Green. I believe in strong individual rights, strong state's rights, and sustainable living. For divisive issues, I intend to experiment with crowd-sourced solutions, notably: liquid feedback, loomio, and citizen juries. More on emerging discourse platforms.
  8. Branching - You are encouraged to use any or all of this platform as a starting point for your own campaign. Everything is available in our GitHub Repo under an MIT license.

Mission Statement - The Third Coalition is primarily concerned with co-ordinating discussion between Third Parties and Independents. We are also on a mission to educate the public about Electoral Reform. Our first challenge is to defeat the entrenched Two-Party System.

About Us - The Direct Democracy Party was founded by Andrew Warshaver and Jeffrey Schroder in late 2014. Our Indiegogo Campaign is still live. Read more about us on our AMA. The Third Coalition is an agile pivot from the original plan.
