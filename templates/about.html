The Direct Democracy Party was co-founded by Andrew Warshaver and Jeffrey Schroder in late 2014. Our Indiegogo Campaign is still live. Read more about us on our AMA.

Mission Statement - The Third Coalition is primarily concerned with co-ordinating discussion between Third Parties and Independents. We are also on a mission to educate the public about Electoral Reform Our first challenge is to defeat the entrenched Two-Party System.

DDP Platform - Runs entirely on the principles of Liquid Democracy. We'll utilize one of the emerging discourse platforms or build our own.

Our Priorities - 1. Return to a Fluid Majority in the House. 2. Enshrine Electoral Reform. 3. Moderate and promote healthy discourse in Congress.
