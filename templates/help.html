The next way to help is get educated on Electoral Reform, and start a discussion in your community. Decide amonst yourselves what your favorite type of reform is. To facilitate orderly, transparent coordination, we are asking every party / independent organization to send 2-5 members to our subreddit, /r/thirdcoalition. We need your help filling out the links, logos, color preferences, and mottos.

Brownie points if one or more of your liasons is able to submit pull requests to our GitHub Repo (or at least works with someone who can). We've made it easy for non-programmers to submit changes.

Start your own primary! You can use our software to run an internal primary, or a similar inter-party movement in another country. Contact Us about getting set up. Alternatively, fork the repo and run your own servers. We are under an MIT license so you are free to go wild.
